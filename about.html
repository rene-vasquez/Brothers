About Brothers Valet

We'll work with you to bring our expertise and your venue together to ensure that your customers walk away with the best possible experience.

Gregory Jacques

Director of Operations

Gregory.Jacques@BrothersValet.com

My name is Gregory Jacques, an Austin-ite who is a Westwood High School Warrior, and a Texas State Bobcat. I started working valet while enrolled in classes back in 2008. After graduating with a BA focused in Mass Communications, I partnered with Kyle Manley to create Brothers Valet back in 2014.

While I do enjoy valeting - discounted meals, greeting guests and building relationships - my passion is creating art, furniture, and any tangible objects with any material I can get my hands on. I aspire to be an artist who uses the resources of the environment, to create art which reflects the culture.

Kyle Manley

General Manager

Kyle.Manley@BrothersValet.com

Kyle Manley was born and raised on a chain of lakes in Saint Cloud Florida and during his time there developed a love for fishing, camping, and any general exploration of new places. Over the years he traveled to many different states and countries allowing him to learn to relate to a variety of people.

Having worked many customer service jobs Kyle cultivated a passion for taking care of people and through that passion finally came to work as a valet, finding an area he truly excelled, making it possible to create a viable business.
